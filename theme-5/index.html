Voice & Tone

Clinical, clear, supportive — for UK haematology practice.

Clinical

Evidence-based, precise, never speculative. Reference BSH, NICE, WHO.

Do: "First-line investigation is ferritin and CRP."
Don't: "You might want to check iron studies."

Clear

Plain English, active voice, short sentences. Define acronyms first use.

Do: "Start oral iron if ferritin <30 µg/L."
Don't: "Consideration could be given to iron replacement therapy."

Supportive

Encouraging and non-judgemental. Built for time-poor clinicians.

Do: "Revise key differentials in 5 minutes."
Don't: "You should already know this."
Principles
Accuracy over speed. Clarity over cleverness. Kindness over ego.
